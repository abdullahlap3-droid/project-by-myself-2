Frequently Asked Questions
How can I book tickets for a show?
Go to the shows page, pick your show, select date and seat, and complete booking.
Can I request a refund after booking?
Refund policy depends on venue and show type. Most requests are accepted before 24 hours.
How do I know if a show is in Urdu, Hindi, or English?
Each show card includes language and runtime details under the title.
Where can I see upcoming events list?
Open the Home and Shows pages for upcoming productions and schedule timeline.
Do you have student discounts?
Selected venues and festival shows include student discount windows.
How can I submit feedback?
Use the form on this page or email support directly using mailto links.
